# Agent Rules

## Ngon ngu trao doi

- Trao doi voi nguoi dung bang tieng Viet, ngan gon va ro y.
- Khi can nhac ky thuat, neu co gia dinh thi noi ro gia dinh do.

## Quy trinh lam viec

1. Doc yeu cau va kiem tra ngu canh lien quan trong repo truoc khi sua.
2. Thuc hien thay doi can thiet, giu pham vi gon dung voi yeu cau.
3. Kiem tra lai bang cach phu hop: doc lai code, chay lenh kiem tra, hoac mo app neu can.
4. Sau khi hoan thanh, phai bao cao cho nguoi dung va cho duyet.
5. Chi khi nguoi dung noi ro da duyet / OK / commit di thi moi commit.
6. Sau khi commit thanh cong, push len GitHub neu remote da cau hinh san.

## Bao cao sau khi lam xong

Moi lan hoan thanh mot prompt co thay doi file, bao cao theo cau truc:

- Da lam gi: tom tat cac file va chuc nang da sua.
- Nguyen nhan: neu la fix loi, neu ro vi sao loi xay ra.
- Cach sua: neu ro logic hoac huong tiep can da ap dung.
- Kiem tra: liet ke lenh da chay hoac cach da xac minh.
- Trang thai: noi ro dang cho nguoi dung duyet, chua commit/chua push.

## Quy tac commit va push

- Khong tu commit hoac push ngay sau khi sua xong.
- Luon cho nguoi dung duyet bao cao truoc.
- Khi duoc duyet, tao commit voi message ngan gon, ro nghia.
- Sau commit, push len branch hien tai bang remote mac dinh.
- Neu push loi, bao cao nguyen nhan va lenh/ket qua quan trong.

## Bao ve thay doi cua nguoi dung

- Khong revert, xoa, format lai cac thay doi khong lien quan.
- Neu working tree dang co thay doi tu truoc, phai bao cho nguoi dung biet truoc khi commit.
- Neu commit, chi dua vao commit cac file lien quan den yeu cau da duyet.
